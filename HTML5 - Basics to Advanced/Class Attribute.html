Netflix Series

"Stranger Things" is set in the 1980s in the small town of Hawkins, Indiana, where a young boy named Will Byers mysteriously vanishes. As his friends and family search for him, they uncover a series of extraordinary events involving a secret government lab, a girl with psychokinetic abilities named Eleven, and a parallel dimension called the Upside Down. The town becomes the epicenter of supernatural occurrences and battles against terrifying creatures from this dark world, blending elements of science fiction, horror, and nostalgic 80s pop culture.

""Dark" is a German science fiction thriller set in the small town of Winden, where the disappearance of two children exposes the town's hidden secrets and the intricate connections between four estranged families. As the investigation unfolds, it reveals a complex time travel conspiracy spanning several generations, with events in 1953, 1986, and 2019 becoming intertwined. The series delves into themes of fate, free will, and the cyclical nature of time, as characters struggle to break free from a tragic and seemingly inevitable destiny.

"The Three-Body Problem" is a science fiction novel by Liu Cixin that begins during China's Cultural Revolution and follows the story of Ye Wenjie, an astrophysicist who makes contact with an alien civilization called the Trisolarans. The Trisolarans, whose home planet is in a state of constant chaos due to the gravitational effects of three suns, plan to invade Earth. As humanity becomes divided between those who welcome the aliens and those who want to resist, the novel explores profound themes of first contact, the clash of civilizations, and the impact of scientific discovery on human society.
