The Poppy War

Inspired by Chinese history, "The Poppy War" follows Rin, a war orphan determined to escape her impoverished existence by earning a place at an elite military academy. As Rin discovers her shamanic powers and delves deeper into the brutal realities of war, R.F. Kuang weaves a narrative that explores the consequences of power and the sacrifices required for survival. The novel's richly detailed world-building immerses readers in a conflict-ridden society where political intrigue and magical warfare collide. Through Rin's journey, the story grapples with themes of identity, nationalism, and the moral complexities of wielding extraordinary abilities in the pursuit of justice and vengeance.
