Years of Rice and Salt

"Years of Rice and Salt" is an alternate history novel that imagines a world where the Black Death decimates Europe, leaving Asia and Islamic cultures to dominate global history. Kim Stanley Robinson explores themes of reincarnation, cultural evolution, and the impact of human choices on history. The novel follows the reincarnated souls of a group of characters across centuries and continents, offering a sweeping panorama of human civilization and its resilience in the face of adversity. Through its exploration of different cultures and historical epochs, "Years of Rice and Salt" challenges readers to reconsider the forces that shape our world and the possibilities of alternative destinies.
