Three Body Problem

This epic trilogy begins with "The Three-Body Problem," where humanity learns of an impending invasion by an alien civilization, setting off a chain of events spanning generations. Liu Cixin weaves a tale that combines hard science with profound philosophical questions about humanity's place in the universe. As the story unfolds, readers encounter mind-bending concepts like the Trisolarans' unpredictable nature and the consequences of their advanced technology. The trilogy explores themes of survival, technological progress, and the moral complexities of contact with extraterrestrial intelligence. Each book builds upon the last, offering a sweeping narrative that challenges perceptions of reality and the future of civilization itself.
