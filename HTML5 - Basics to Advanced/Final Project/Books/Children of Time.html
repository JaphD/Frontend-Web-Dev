Children of Time

"Children of Time" unfolds across generations as humanity faces extinction on a dying Earth and attempts to colonize a new planet terraformed with a nanovirus that local species. Adrian Tchaikovsky's novel explores themes of evolution, intelligence, and the consequences of hubris as the inhabitants of the planet—now populated by highly evolved spiders—encounter the last remnants of humanity. The narrative alternates between the perspectives of spiders and humans, challenging preconceptions about intelligence and societal development. "Children of Time" is a thought-provoking exploration of the symbiotic relationship between species and the potential paths of evolution in an ever-changing universe.
